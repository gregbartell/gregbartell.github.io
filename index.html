Washington Special License Plate Collection

Welcome to my dumb hobby site!
I have some sort of brain worm that causes me to think it's fun to take photographs of the different Washington license plate backgrounds. Sometimes my similarly-afflicted friends send me their own photos and I put those up here too. When I get duplicate photos, I generally try to use the highest-quality one with the lowest assigned number or an otherwise interesting one. There are no real hard and fast rules, it's whatever I like the best.
